properties

Maracuya (Passion Fruit): Rich in vitamin C, promoting a healthy immune system. Contains dietary fiber, supporting digestive health. High antioxidant content may contribute to skin health. Mango: High in vitamins A and C, beneficial for vision and immune system. Contains enzymes like amylases, aiding in digestion. Provides a good amount of fiber for digestive health. Manzana (Apple): High in dietary fiber, supporting digestive health. Contains antioxidants, which may contribute to heart health. Apples are known to help regulate blood sugar levels. Piña (Pineapple): Rich in vitamin C, supporting immune health. Contains bromelain, an enzyme with anti-inflammatory properties. High water content helps in hydration. Melon: Excellent source of hydration due to high water content. Contains vitamins A and C, benefiting skin and immune system. Provides potassium, supporting heart health. Papaya: Rich in vitamin C and A, promoting immune and skin health. Contains enzymes like papain, aiding in digestion. High fiber content supports digestive health. Fresa (Strawberry): High in vitamin C and manganese, supporting immune health. Rich in antioxidants, which may contribute to heart health. Contains anthocyanins, beneficial for skin health. Arándanos (Blueberries): Packed with antioxidants, supporting heart and brain health. Rich in vitamin C and fiber, promoting overall health. Contains anthocyanins, beneficial for eye health. Sandía (Watermelon): High water content contributes to hydration. Contains antioxidants like lycopene, supporting heart health. Provides vitamins A and C, beneficial for skin and immune system. Coco (Coconut): Rich in medium-chain fatty acids, supporting heart health. Contains electrolytes, promoting hydration. Provides fiber, supporting digestive health. Uvas (Grapes): High in antioxidants, benefiting heart health. Contains resveratrol, which may have anti-aging properties. Provides natural sugars for a quick energy boost. Mora (Blackberry): Rich in vitamins C and K, supporting immune and bone health. High in fiber, promoting digestive health. Contains antioxidants, beneficial for overall health. Kiwi: Excellent source of vitamin C, supporting immune health. Provides vitamin K, beneficial for blood clotting and bone health. High fiber content supports digestive health. Pitaya Amarilla y Roja (Yellow and Red Dragon Fruit): Rich in vitamin C, promoting immune health. Contains antioxidants, which may have anti-inflammatory properties. Provides iron and fiber, supporting overall health. Lichi (Lychee): High in vitamin C, supporting immune health. Contains B-vitamins, promoting energy metabolism. Rich in antioxidants, beneficial for skin health.
